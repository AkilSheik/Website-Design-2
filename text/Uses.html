Uses

The cloud can be used for a vairety of things, but its primary use would be to store data. With the cloud, you can store your pictures, make a backup, store applications that you are creating, and do many other things without using your own storage. Once you have stored your data on the cloud, you can access it anywhere, provided that the cloud service is not blocked and that there is internet, making it very useful.
