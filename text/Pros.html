Pros

The cloud has many uses that allow it to be such a powerful and useful tool. You can make backups of your hard drive or photos, so you do not have to worry about losing it if your computer breaks. You could access information on the go on less powerful laptops, so you are not restricted to working in a set place. It also can allow easy editing of data over multiple devices.
