How Can You Get It?

So now that you are intrigued by the Cloud, you may be asking yourself, how can I get it? Well, there are many ways to cure that curiousity. Many companies, such as Google, Microsoft, and Apple offer cloud services that you can pay to use, and if you would like to try it first, you can usually obtain a free trial. All you have to do to get a cloud service is to go on the internet and search. If you would like to make your own cloud, you can also do that by making your own server. However, you risk exposing yourself to malvalent hackers if you decide to do so. The choice is yours.
