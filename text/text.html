What is Cloud Computing?

Cloud Computing, is simply using another computer over your internet to store and use data. So, instead of using your own computer and having your data stuck on one network, you get the benefit of having it be on a remote server. This means that you can access your data or applications virtually anywhere, as long as there is internet. As you can imagine, this is very useful, hence why it is so popular.
