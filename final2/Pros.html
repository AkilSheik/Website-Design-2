Pros

The cloud has many uses that allow it to be such a powerful and useful tool. You can make backups of your hard drive or photos, so you do not have to worry about losing it if your computer breaks. You could access information on the go on less powerful laptops, so you are not restricted to working in a set place. It also can allow easy editing of data over multiple devices. In addition, it helps enterprises a lot, by making them not need any servers to manage, having little downtime, and it being rather reliable, as providers would have to take precautions such as backups to safeguard their data, or risk their reputation. The cloud is limitless, much like the sky.

Recap

  • It allows easy editing
  • It can backup files
  • It eliminates the need for servers to be managed
  • It does not have too much downtime and is rather reliable
  • Data is usually safe
